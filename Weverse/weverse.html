Help Center · Quick Start

Simple guidance for new users in Hong Kong and Mainland China, including registration, payment and event participation.

How do I join the June campaign?
Sign up or log in, then follow the “June campaign” entry on the homepage or Fandom Hub. All related missions, events and shop items are tagged accordingly.
How do I switch language and region?
Use the language toggle in the top-right and select your region in Settings. The website automatically adjusts time formats and currency displays.
Payment & shipping
We support major international payment methods and selected local options. Shipping is available to Hong Kong and Mainland China with clear delivery estimates.
